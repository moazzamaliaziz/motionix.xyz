# Security

If you found a vulnerability, please *don't* open a public issue.

Email **security@motionix.xyz** with:

- A short description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce (a screenshot is great)
- What you'd expect to happen vs. what does happen
- What version of Motionix you were using (commit SHA, release tag, or "latest")

We aim to acknowledge security emails within 48 hours and to ship a fix
within a week for high-severity reports. We'll coordinate disclosure
timing with you if it's not critical.

## Safe harbor

We will not pursue legal action against you for security research
conducted in good faith on Motionix.xyz properties. Please avoid:

- Privacy violations (no pulling customer data)
- Service degradation (no DDoS, no spam)
- Anything illegal under your jurisdiction or ours
